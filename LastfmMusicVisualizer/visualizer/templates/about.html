About This Project

What Is the Last.fm Visualizer?

The Last.fm Visualizer is a tool designed to transform your Last.fm listening history into unique, striking visualizations. Whether you're curious about your top artists, long-term listening trends, or daily listening habits, this tool provides an insightful way to explore your musical identity.

How It Works

  • You enter your Last.fm username.
  • The site requests your public scrobble data from the Last.fm API.
  • You are taken to a stats page that summarizes your listening history.
  • After viewing the stats, you select a visualization type and choose any filters or customization options.
  • After submitting your choices, you are taken to the visualization page, where your custom visualization is generated and displayed.

No account on this site is required to view your stats or generate visualizations. However, if you want to save your generated visualizations for later access, you’ll need to be logged in to a site account.

Get Started
